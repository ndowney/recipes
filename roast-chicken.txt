Roast chicken recipe

Get a chicken. like 3-5 pounds weight.

Put a lot of salt on it, like rub it all over with kosher salt so it is covered.

Let it sit , covered, in the fridge for 2 days.

preheat oven to 500F

Take it out, pat dry with paper towel.

heat oven proof pan on stove until hot

put chicken on pan, you should hear a sizzle.

Then put chicken in oven.  Flip after 30min, then back after another 20.  Cook for one hour total.

Take chicken out.

Enjoy
